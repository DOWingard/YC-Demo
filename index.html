Projections

I have a 10-year projection that scales globally (US, China, Mexico, Japan) at reasonable growth. Virality will only accelerate it.

10-Year Global Scaling Financial Projection

Summary of assumptions, growth, revenue, costs, and profitability metrics (monthly resolution).

1. User Growth

Initial user base: 50 active users.

Target user base: ~1,000,000 users by Year 5; ~2,000,000 users by Year 10.

Growth pattern: Smooth and consistent global scaling — accelerating early adoption and gradually slowing as the platform reaches widespread adoption.

2. Revenue Assumptions

  • ARPU: $50 / month
  • Revenue behavior: Scales proportionally with active users.
  • Growth dynamics: No sudden spikes — steady increase with user adoption.

3. Cost Assumptions

  • Cloud infrastructure and hosting
  • Team salaries and personnel costs
  • Marketing and operational expenses

Behavior: Costs increase initially with growth, then flatten with scale.

4. Profitability Metrics

Net profit = Total revenue − Total costs (per period).

Cumulative profit = Sum of net profit over time (monthly aggregation).

5. Simplifying Assumptions

  • No explicit user churn or region-specific adoption differences.
  • Constant ARPU over time.
  • Uniform global adoption.
  • Costs flatten naturally with growth.

Notes

This projection is conservative and simplified to support high-level planning.
