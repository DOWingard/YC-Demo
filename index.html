Projections

I have a 10-year projection that scales globally (US, China, Mexico, Japan) at reasonable growth. Virality will only accelerate it.

Assumptions:

Over 10 years, the platform scales from 50 to about 2 million users globally, earning roughly $50 per user monthly, with steadily increasing revenue and flattening costs as operations mature. Costs grow early due to infrastructure, salaries, and marketing but stabilize over time, yielding gradual, sustainable profitability under conservative growth assumptions.
